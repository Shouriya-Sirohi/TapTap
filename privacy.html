Privacy Policy for TapTap

Last updated: 27 January 2026

TapTap (“the App”) is a brand and property of Chimera Techsphere (OPC) Pvt. Ltd., incorporated under the laws of India. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

TapTap does not directly collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information for functionality, analytics, and monetization.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

TapTap displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process:

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data
  • IP address (for ad serving and fraud prevention)

This information may be used to:

  • Show personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud
  • Improve ad quality

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, personalized advertising is shown only after obtaining user consent where required by law.

Users may change or withdraw their consent at any time within the app’s privacy settings.

TapTap does not control or access the data collected by Google AdMob.

Learn more about how Google uses data:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Analytics and Firebase

TapTap uses Firebase services (by Google) for:

  • App stability
  • Crash reporting
  • Basic usage analytics

Firebase may collect anonymized data such as:

  • App performance information
  • Crash logs
  • Device and OS information

This data is used solely to improve app stability and user experience.

Firebase Privacy Policy:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where required by applicable law, data processing for advertising and analytics is based on user consent.

Users may withdraw consent at any time through the app’s privacy settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

TapTap is not specifically designed for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and it will be removed.

6. Data Sharing

TapTap:

  • Does NOT sell user data
  • Does NOT independently share personal data
  • Uses third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase) as described above

7. Internet Permission

The App requires internet access to load advertisements and essential services.

No personal user-generated content is transmitted.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
